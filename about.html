Our Story

Built on trust, one shift at a time

The Idea

MoveMitra began as a small local transport crew, frustrated by how unpredictable and overpriced household shifting had become for ordinary families.

Going Door-to-Door

We rebuilt our process around a simple promise: pick up from your door, deliver to the new one, with one team and one point of contact throughout.

Expanding Services

As more customers asked for help beyond furniture, we added vehicle transport and small goods courier — all under the same safe-packing, honest-pricing standard.

Today

MoveMitra now serves 120+ cities with a trained, verified crew and a customer-first approach that's earned us a loyal base of repeat customers.
